PHONE CARE SECRETS.

Prevention is the best repair. Follow these technician-approved habits to double your device's lifespan.

Extend Battery Life

Most users charge to 100%, but this actually stresses the lithium-ion cells over time.

Tech Secret

Keep your charge between 20% and 80% to reduce cycle wear by up to 40%.

Avoid Screen Damage

It's not just about drops. Micro-scratches from sand and keys weaken the glass structure.

Tech Secret

Never clean your screen with glass cleaner (Windex). It strips the oleophobic coating!

Charging Mistakes

Using cheap cables is the #1 reason logic boards fail due to voltage spikes.

Tech Secret

Avoid "Fast Charging" overnight. The heat generated degrades the logic board components.
